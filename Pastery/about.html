Crafted with love, baked with care

Founded in 2020, My Bakery began as a small passion project and grew into a place celebrated for elegant pastries, artisan breads, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Every morning starts before sunrise, as our team mixes, proofs, and bakes each item by hand with care and patience. We believe the finest details make the sweetest experience.

From delicate pastries to signature desserts, every creation is made to bring comfort, flavor, and a touch of delight to your day.

Our Story

We honor traditional baking methods while adding a modern touch, creating treats that feel timeless, polished, and unmistakably special.
